Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is EcoActionTracker?

EcoActionTracker helps you track your daily eco-friendly activities to contribute to a sustainable future.

2. How can I get started?

Simply create an account on our website and start tracking your actions today! You can log your activities to monitor your impact.

3. How do I contact support?

You can reach us by email at ecoactiontracker53@gmail.com. We’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

4. Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time by contacting support. Please refer to the privacy policy for more details.

5. Is there a mobile app for EcoActionTracker?

Currently, we only offer a web-based platform, but we’re working on making the app available soon!
