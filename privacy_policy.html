Privacy Policy

Introduction

At EcoActionTracker, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information when you use our website. Since we use **local storage** to save your data, no personal data is stored on our servers.

Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data, but all data is stored locally in your browser's **local storage**:

  • Personal Information: The data you provide when creating an account, such as your name, email, and activity logs, is stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: We track your interactions with the platform to improve your user experience. This data is also stored locally in your browser.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies to enhance your experience on our site. You can manage cookie settings through your browser preferences.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data stored in **local storage** to provide a personalized and seamless experience, including:

  • Tracking your eco-friendly actions and progress.
  • Personalizing your user experience based on past interactions.
  • Remembering your preferences across sessions without needing to log in.

Data Security

Since your data is stored in **local storage**, it is only accessible on your device. We do not store any personal information on our servers, reducing the risk of unauthorized access. However, we recommend securing your device with strong passwords or biometric authentication to protect your data.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or sell any of the data stored in your **local storage**. Your data is kept entirely on your device. We only access the data in your local storage to provide the services you use.

Your Rights

As your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can:

  • Clear your browser's local storage at any time to delete all saved data.
  • Control the cookies used on the site through your browser settings.
  • Opt-out of any personalized features by disabling local storage or using incognito mode in your browser.

Contact Us
